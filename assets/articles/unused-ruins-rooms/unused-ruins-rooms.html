Collapsed Floor

This is room 261 in v2.4.3b. It was meant to be placed between the "Lone Frog" room and the rock puzzle on this page.

In the room, there's a sign that reads:

Most of the floor has, in fact collapsed. There's only a narrow path remaining that goes to the exit and it's possible to fall off, in which case you will be teleported back to the start.

Spikes block the exit. It's not possible to deactivate them, but noclipping through them leads you to the rock puzzle room.

"Rock Puzzle"

This is room 26 in v2.4.3b. It was meant to be placed between the aforementioned collapsed floor room and "Mouse Hole" save point.

Inside it is a sign that reads:

There are a set of spikes blocking the way and a button, which can only be pressed by a rock.

To progress, the player must use the Rock vessel to press the button.

Exiting this room through the right puts you near the Mouse Hole save point.

Through the left exit, the room connects to the room now named "Lone Frog"
