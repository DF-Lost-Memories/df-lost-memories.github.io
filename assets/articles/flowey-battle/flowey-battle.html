Room 150 in v1.3.3 is a battle against Flowey.

The music used is Your Best Friend. It stops playing on turn 6

In the normal state

Checking or sparing him progresses the fight normally.

Missing an attack or using an item will cause him to react with special lines, but won't progress the fight.

Flowey doesn't attack on his first turn. On every other turn, he shoots friendliness pellets at the player.

Touching a pellet crashes the game.

Dealing damage to him causes him to get angry (see the other state). He will also react with a different line depending on whether he's attacked on the first turn or anytime else.

Attacking and dealing 0 damage makes him switch to his angry sprite, changes the music pitch, and triggers the angry state flavor text. However, the fight still progresses as in the normal state.

Flowey can be killed or spared when he is sparing the player.

In the angry state

The music is pitched down.

Flowey uses a faster version of the attack pattern from his normal state.

Touching a pellet instantly kills the player and brings up the normal game over screen.

Missing or using an item causes him to react with special lines.

Attacking him makes him react as if you attacked him for the first time, most likely due to an oversight.

This state is essentially a softlock as Flowey can't be defeated and doesn't go back to th enormal state.

(Note: "Turn" in the next sections only refers to turns where the battle progresses, aka when the player checks or spares)
