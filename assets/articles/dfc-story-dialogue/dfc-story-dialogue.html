As shown in the February 2021 Progress Report, DF CONNECTED v2.5.0 was meant to launch with a playable story. This obviously never came to be, but lines of dialogue were still left over in the code.

While I originally discovered these while looking through the game's strings, it turns out a lot of them are accessible in older versions by going out of bounds!

Flowey (main quest)

First meeting

This can be accessed in the Flowey room by going out of bounds to the right. A variant of the dialogue can be seen by triggering the interaction from above.

Second meeting

This can be accessed in front of Toriel's house, again to the right out of bounds, but only after triggering the first meeting.

Third meeting

This isn't accessible ingame, but it probably would've taken place at the same spot as the second meeting after defeating Mandarina.

Others

Blocked by strings

One of these interactions is placed behind the gate that goes to the Ruins from Snowdin.
The others are meant to be in front of Toriel's house (see the video about the second Flowey interaction).

This is the only out of bounds interaction on this page that's still accessible today.

Fun fact: Before it was merged with this page, this interaction was also the first article on the website!

Random silkworm

This is triggered out of bounds in the Lone Hall room.
