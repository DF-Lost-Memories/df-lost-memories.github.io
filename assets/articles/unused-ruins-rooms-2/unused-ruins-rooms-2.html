Rock Room

Room 210 in v2.5.2 or 265 in v2.7.12 is a rock pushing puzzle room that would've been placed between the then-unused second rock puzzle and Leaf Pile rooms.

The room consists of a rectangular area almost completely filled with pushable rocks. The player must push some of the rocks to the 3 buttons while also making sure to leave a path to move forward, as it is possible to get softlocked in this room.

While the rest of this part of the Ruins below the fountain was finally made accessible in v2.7.9, this room was still left unused.

Spike Bridge

Room 41 in v2.5.2 and v2.7.8b, or 46 in v2.7.12, is a room where the player has to traverse a bridge made out of spikes over a body of water.

The spikes will all activate once the player approaches the bridge. They will then start deactivating in a certain pattern which seems to be rather buggy, thus preventing the player from progressing.

It is possible, although difficult, to complete this room if starting from the other end of the bridge, however.
