Okay, what's THIS?

The secret flipside of Lost Memories that focuses on content that's exclusive to the 2018 demo...?

But why is it completely separate from everything

Simply put, Demo 2 is a completely different game than everything that came after it, and has way too much unused content that's exclusive to itself.

Since almost none of it was carried over to future versions and almost everything was related to the full game that Demo 2 was intended to become, I decided to separate these articles into a separate page instead of flooding the main page with them.

Things that originated from Demo 2 but are still present in later versions are still listed in Lost Memories.

Are the rules the same?

Well, I'm glad you asked! They aren't.

  • Content ripped straight from the game files IS allowed. The unencrypted data.win is publicly available, and RickyG has given permission for content to be shared from it.
  • Modding is also allowed for documentation purposes. However, you can't connect this version to GMS, and you can't distribute mods.

Okay, that's it. Have fun!
